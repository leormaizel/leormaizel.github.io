about me

Hi, I’m Leor, a Product Designer from Seattle. I have over 9 years of experience with companies ranging in size from seed stage to Fortune 500, in sectors such as FinTech, Travel, Civic Tech, to Commercial Real Estate.

I’m an interdisciplinary designer. A generalist. I enjoy being involved in all stages of the product lifecycle. I’m not a design purist. I’m not a stickler for process or artifact. I bias toward action and often prefer to see a feature released and battle-tested than brought to pixel-perfection. Some of the principles that have helped me most in my career are:

  • The 80/20 rule, whose wisdom I’m reminded of continually
  • The words of Reid Hoffman: “​​If you are not embarrassed by the first version of your product, you’ve launched too late”
  • There are often many good design solutions to a problem, so best not to be too rigid in your opinion
  • Often, the loudest and most confident voices prevail, but the most sustainable solutions are almost always the result of patience, listening, and consensus
